How does the AI System work?

Makes a call

AI System makes an automatic call to every phone number on the database. It is capable of calling 100,000 subscribers per minute. You can adjust the speed of the calls, the number of repeated calls on each number, and the frequency of repeated calls if the subscriber didn’t accept the call.

Communicates

AI System plays the message to a subscriber.

You can record the scenario on your own to a phone and upload it to the system.

AI System plays the message to a subscriber.

You can also use our professional narrators service.

Moves a deal to the next stage

The AI System takes the next step in the algorithm according to the subscriber’s action.

It can be:

  • putting a call through to a human manager
  • sending an SMS or a message via a messenger
  • recording data of the deal’s profile (name, date, time)
  • creating a deal or a task in the CRM system.

Forms the statistics

You will be able to evaluate the efficacy of the automatic calls in action with the help of such tools as:

  • Clicks report
  • Goals summary report
  • Expenses report.

What the AI System is capable of doing?

  • Plays messages
  • Supports a dialog according to the predefined scenarios
  • Puts a call through to any number
  • Sends SMS messages after the call to e-mail, WhatsApp, etc.
  • Before connecting to the subscriber it displays a message to the operator in the form of the text of the previous conversation with the subscriber through speech synthesizer
  • Creates goals for the calls and monitors their achievement
  • Sends statistics of responses online mode
  • Gathers and uses data (name, date, time, etc.) during the calls, records it in the profile of a call, and forwards it to a human manager using an e-mail, messenger, and SMS.

FAQ

You can receive clients’ answers by clicking the phone buttons or by using a voice recognition. The robot not only recognizes the answers – but it also records them and turns them into system commands.

Integration with a third-party service is made via API. Learn the documentation or contact our specialist.

The money stays on the account in the system until you address the service again.

You can record the text used for calls using a dictaphone and insert it into a special block. It is also possible to use a voice synthesizer. To do that, simply paste the text into a special field so a robot can voice it. You can also use the services of our professional narrators.

The payment for each call is withdrawn after the call is completed. As soon as a person picks up the phone, the payment for the connection is withdrawn. Afterwards you will be charged for the traffic, according to the chosen base rate.

We do not provide any databases for the use of voice spamming. According to paragraph 6.1. of the AI System Service Offer, no data entered by a user is available (including the personal data of message recipients and the content of the information sent by users).
